## Format quirks (hard-won, don't rediscover)
- MDB: metric x10 ints; solar = lux x10 (/126.7 → W/m²); UV = µW/cm² (/450
  → index); 32767/127/255 are sensor-dead sentinels; TIME is local.
- WU: pre-2019 records use millisecond epochs; qcStatus 0 = drop.
- Cumulus monthly logs: column count grows 17→27 over the years (prefix of
  monthlyfileheader.txt); alltimelog.txt is not a monthly log.
- fnm requires PowerShell (not git-bash) for node/npm/npx.
